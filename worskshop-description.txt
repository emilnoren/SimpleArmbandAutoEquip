[h2]Description[/h2]
This mod automatically equips units with armbands from [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=2778578325]Jedis Simple Armbands mod[/url]. 
Faction specific options for color, size and occurence.

The script minimal and only runs on unit creation, so it will have no impact on performance. 
This is a quick mod I threw together for our Antistasi Ultimate server, so use as is.
